Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 (12 ounce) package Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until the meat is browned. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and oregano and stir until well combined and heated through.
  2. Stir mozzarella, cottage cheese, and parmesan together in a large bowl.
  3. Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of a slow cooker. Add a double layer of uncooked lasagna noodles, breaking noodles to fit into cooker as needed. Top noodles with a portion of cheese mixture. Reapeat the layering until all the ingredients are used.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 4 to 6 hours.
